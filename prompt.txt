Scoring Architecture - 100 points across 3 tiers

FOUNDATION - 40 pts
Emergency Fund - 15 pts
Compute emRatio = actual / ideal from the existing computeEmergency helper. Score as follows:

emRatio ≥ 2.0 → 15
emRatio ≥ 1.0 → 12
emRatio ≥ 0.75 → 9
emRatio ≥ 0.5 → 6
emRatio ≥ 0.25 → 3
emRatio < 0.25 → 1

Insurance Coverage - 15 pts
Split into health (8 pts) and life (7 pts), scored independently using coverage ratios from computeInsurance.
Health scoring (8 pts):

Coverage ≥ ideal → 8
Coverage ≥ 50% of ideal → 5
Coverage < 50% → 2

Life scoring (7 pts):

idealLife = 0 (no dependants) → automatic 7, life insurance not required
Coverage ≥ ideal → 7
Coverage ≥ 50% of ideal → 4
Coverage < 50% → 1

Final insurance score = healthPts + lifePts.
Liability Management - 10 pts
Use computeLiabilities to get good debt, bad debt, and EMI figures. Compute emiRatio = totalMonthlyEMI / monthlyIncome.

No liabilities at all → 10
Only good debt AND emiRatio ≤ 40% → 10
Only good debt AND emiRatio > 40% → 4
Good debt outstanding > bad debt outstanding AND emiRatio ≤ 40% → 7
Good debt outstanding > bad debt outstanding AND emiRatio > 40% → 4
Bad debt present, emiRatio ≤ 20% → 4
Bad debt present, emiRatio > 20% → 2


BEHAVIOUR - 40 pts

sipRatio 5–10% → 6

Fewer than 3 months → multiply by 0.8
Field does not exist on p → multiply by 1.0, do not penalise

Final score = Math.round(basePoints × multiplier).

After summing all category scores, compute the penalty and subtract from the total.
Define:

zeroEmergency = emergencyFund score ≤ 1
zeroInsurance = insurance score ≤ 2
highBadDebt = bad debt outstanding > 30% of annual income

Apply the first matching condition only:

All three true → penalty = 15, flag = critical_triple_gap
zeroEmergency + zeroInsurance → penalty = 8, flag = no_emergency_no_insurance
zeroEmergency + highBadDebt → penalty = 6, flag = no_emergency_high_debt
zeroInsurance + highBadDebt → penalty = 5, flag = no_insurance_high_debt
None match → penalty = 0, flags = []

totalScore = Math.min(100, Math.max(0, sumOfAllCategories - penalty))
